Our Story

A Journey of Flavors Anchored in Memory

Farro was founded on the principle that dining should be an act of storytelling. Our Middle Eastern-inspired cuisine reimagines heritage recipes through a modern lens, using locally-sourced, high-quality ingredients.

"With FARRO, we've envisioned more than just a restaurant; it is a space of connection and celebration. Every detail is designed to make diners feel part of a story."

Aman Talreja, Co-Founder
